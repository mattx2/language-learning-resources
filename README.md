# Language Learning Resources

A curated collection of guides, comparisons, and practical advice for anyone learning a new language in 2026. Whether you're picking up your first foreign language or adding a fifth, these resources will help you make informed decisions about methods, platforms, and how to turn language skills into career advantages.

Maintained by [Class Coupon](https://classcoupon.com) — a platform that helps learners find discounts on online classes, certifications, and language courses.

---

## Guides

### [Choosing a Language Learning Platform](guides/choosing-a-language-platform.md)
A side-by-side comparison of six popular platforms — Preply, LTL Flexi Classes, Cambly, Duolingo, Babbel, and Rosetta Stone — covering pricing, teaching style, and who each one works best for.

### [Language Learning Methods Compared](guides/language-learning-methods-compared.md)
Immersion, classroom instruction, mobile apps, and private tutoring all have trade-offs. This guide breaks down what the research says about each approach and helps you figure out which combination fits your goals.

### [Career Benefits of Multilingualism](guides/career-benefits-of-multilingualism.md)
From salary premiums to industries actively hiring multilingual professionals, this guide covers the concrete career advantages of speaking more than one language — and how to certify your skills so employers take notice.
